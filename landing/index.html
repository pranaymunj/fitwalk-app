How It Works

1

Walk Your Route

Start walk tracking inside the app. Take a circular walk around your neighborhood blocks.

2

Close the Loop

Return to within 30 meters of your starting point to close the tracking path loop.

3

Claim Territory

Submit your loop to claim all enclosing H3 grid tiles under your color and score area points.

Game Features

⬡

Capture Territory

Claim H3 grid tiles (resolution 10) by walking enclosed loops. Tiles are filled with your unique player color on the map.

🛡️

Base Protection (Roots)

Your primary home base cell and the surrounding 2 grid rings are protected. Competitor bots cannot steal them.

⚔️

Tile Stealing

Encircle competitor bot tiles to steal their territory and overwrite them under your color.

📈

Stats Tracker

View your total claimed area, recorded walk histories, and path stats inside a clean, premium dashboard.

🏆

Live Leaderboard

Compete against local AI bots in a real-time leaderboard ranking sorted by total square meters of claimed territory.

⏱️

Lazy Tile Decay

Territory isn't permanent. Claims older than 7 days decay and revert to neutral unless walked and claimed again.
